## About Me

I am a computational physicist with expertise in **excited-state dynamics**, 
**potential energy surface construction**, and **quantum/classical simulation methods**. 
My work spans from developing in-house C codes for non-adiabatic molecular dynamics, 
to fitting interatomic potentials from ab initio reference data, to incorporating 
nuclear quantum effects via the adaptive Quantum Thermal Bath (adQTB) method.

I am motivated by problems that sit at the interface of quantum and classical 
descriptions of matter — where electronic degrees of freedom actively shape 
nuclear dynamics.
